## Title

Congratulations to our "wa" team for winning second prize in the NAIC 2025 AI+image coding track!

# Content

The finals of this year's National Artificial Intelligence Challenge (NAIC) were contested at the Shenzhen-Pengcheng Laboratory.
After a week of fierce competition, our "wa" team came out on top and won the second prize!

Our "wa" team consists of four members: Yiheng Jiang (captain), Yuqi Li, Yifan Bian, and Haotian Zhang.
During the competition, the team members cooperated with each other, overcame the difficulties, 
and finally delivered a satisfactory solution to the AI+image coding task in the NPU environment.
